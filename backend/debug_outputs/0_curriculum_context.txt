Topic : Subtraction of Three digit numbers 
context : In this stage, students practice subtracting larger numbers where each digit in the top number (minuend) is greater than or equal to the digit below it (subtrahend). This reinforces place value alignment (Hundreds, Tens, Ones) without the complexity of regrouping.
Learning Outcome : 
1. Students will perform 3-digit subtraction by aligning digits according to their place value. 
2. Students will master the concept of borrowing from the Tens place to the Ones, and from the Hundreds place to the Tens. 
3. Students will solve word problems involving the subtraction of 3-digit numbers.
Examples : 
1. Vertical Subtraction:

   6  7  8
-  2  3  5

   4  4  3
